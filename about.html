About

AnimalMath.com provides free, customizable math worksheet generators for children in Pre-K through 2nd grade. Our tools are designed for teachers, parents, and homeschool educators who want high-quality, printable math practice materials without cost or sign-up barriers.

Every worksheet generator lets you customize number ranges, problem types, and layout settings, then instantly preview and download professional PDFs with answer keys included.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AnimalMath.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

Information we do NOT collect: We do not require user accounts, logins, or registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Automatically collected information: When you visit our site, certain technical information may be collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, including IP address (which may be anonymized), browser type and version, device type and screen resolution, pages visited and time spent, referring website, and approximate geographic location (country/region level).

Third-party advertising: We use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdSense) that may collect information through cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve relevant ads. These third parties may collect device identifiers, browsing history across websites, and information used to infer interests for ad targeting.

How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to understand how our site is used so we can improve it, to serve relevant advertising that supports our ability to offer free tools, and to monitor site performance and prevent abuse.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and ad personalization, which you can manage through our cookie consent mechanism.
  • Legitimate interest: For essential site functionality, security, and analytics that help us improve our free educational tools.

Your Rights

Under GDPR (EEA, UK, Switzerland residents): You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, and port your personal data. You may also object to processing and withdraw consent at any time.

Under CCPA/CPRA (California residents): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete your personal information, to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information. To the extent that personalized advertising constitutes "sharing" under the CCPA, you may opt out via our cookie settings or by enabling the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal in your browser.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below.
