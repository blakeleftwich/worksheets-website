A: Modal Preview (Teachers Pay Teachers style)
Click a card to open a modal with all pages in a 5-column thumbnail grid. Click a thumbnail to see it larger. Download button at the bottom. This is the most common pattern on educational worksheet sites.
+ Users expect this pattern from TPT, Education.com, SuperTeacherWorksheets
+ All 5 pages visible at once — easy to scan
+ Can see a large version of any page before downloading
- Requires an extra click to see previews

B: Lightbox Gallery
Click a card to open a full-screen dark overlay with left/right navigation arrows. Thumbnails at bottom. Like a photo gallery. Common on sites with visual content (Etsy, Pinterest).
+ Maximum focus on the worksheet — no distractions
+ Left/right arrows feel natural for flipping through pages
- Can only see one page at a time
- Feels more like viewing photos than browsing a pack

C: Inline Expand (Accordion)
Click a card and the preview expands directly below it on the page — no overlay or new page. Shows 5 thumbnail pages inline. Used by some modern SaaS sites and app stores.
+ No context switch — stays on the same page
+ Can compare multiple sets easily
- Pushes content below the card down
- Thumbnails are small without a "zoom" option
